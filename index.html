Social Media Privacy Policy

This application is intended for academic, personal, or internal business use only. It does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

Data Access

The application may access publicly available content from Facebook or Instagram Pages that the user manages, such as post messages, engagement metrics (likes and comments), and timestamps. This access is strictly limited to authorized users and approved permissions. No private profile or individual user data is accessed.

Data Use

All accessed data is used solely for academic or business insight purposes. The application does not store this data persistently, nor is it shared with third parties.
